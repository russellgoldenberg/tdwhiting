What Is Executive Coaching?

Individualized coaching provides a one-on-one development opportunity tailored to the specific goals of the executive. Each session begins and ends with a clearly defined action plan to maximize time and results.

The Coaching Process:
An executive coaching process consists of scheduled meetings between the coach and the executive over an agreed-upon period of time. This process includes a number of consulting methods. Examples: needs assessment, definition of expectations, skill practices, videotaping, mentoring, modeling behaviors and 360 feedback.

Benefits of Executive Coaching:

Identify performance barriers to success.
 Increase awareness of verbal and nonverbal communication skills.
 Develop leadership skills.
 Create vision with ability to implement.
 Improve self-discipline and confidence.
 Hone people skills for growth and mentoring.
