What is Life Coaching?
A life coach is a mentor, motivator, planner and consultant rolled into one. It is a person who provides unconditional support to help you with life’s transitions, specific goals, projects, personal and professional relationships and career aspirations.
Coaching is different from therapy. Therapy focuses on your past history, wounds and emotional triggers and behaviors. Coaching deals with the present, today’s issues, situations, concerns and challenges in an effort to focus, balance and move toward positive changes in your life. You are in control with an experienced objective advisor who listens, make suggestions and assists with an action plan and encouragement to succeed.
