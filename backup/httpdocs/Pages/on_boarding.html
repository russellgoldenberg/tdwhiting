On-Board Executive Coaching

Why this is absolutely essential
The process of determining and hiring a senior executive is woefully inadequate when that process stops before a thorough, intelligent orientation or ‘on boarding’ is properly carried out.  To understand why, simply ask yourself these questions:
  • What are the ramifications of a poor decision by a new senior executive? 
  • What are the risks when a new senior executive doesn’t have the critical information (or the right information!) regarding your company’s strategic goals, structure, culture and decision-making process? 
  • What are the actual costs of a failed leader -- even beyond recruitment, search fee, hiring bonus, stock options, salary and severance?  Those numbers alone are staggering. But what of the after effects?  Or the burden on the successor? Or disillusioned staff? Or disappointed customers? Or failed mergers? The list goes on and on.

It’s all about alignment
Simply put, new senior executives need to effectively integrate into their organizations in order to make critical business decisions and execute key strategies aligned to business goals. Without this, new leaders can make decisions that are too hasty, delayed or over reactive. Again, the ramifications are profound.

Avoiding the Pitfalls of Politics and of a Limited Landscape View
At Whiting Consulting, we assist newly hired senior executives in expediting their assimilation into your culture. The ability to transcend one organization to another requires support and a level of objectivity to avoid the pitfalls of politics and a misunderstanding of the new landscape. A neutral third party professional, your onboard coach will clarify expectations and help with transitions and difficult issues in a focused fashion.

Our role is to:

  • Provide a 90-day game plan for success
  • Clarify role and expectations from immediate manager
  • Determine strengths of individual
  • Identify areas for improvement
  • Provide continuous and specific feedback
  • Strengthen communication skills e.g.: influencing, negotiating, meeting management, professional style
  • Accelerate assimilation into culture
