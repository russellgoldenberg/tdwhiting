Mini Series

They say that time changes things, but you have to change them for yourself.
- Andy Warhol


Change in communication and technology is constant. Effective communication is vital in business. Professionals must carve out time to stay current and learn the most efficient and effective way to get the job done.

What is a Mini Series?

An alternative to a 2-day training program, a Mini Series is a 90 – 120 minute program offered during any time of the day that you choose. Designed to introduce a new skill, tip, tool or technique, topics vary and are mainly focused on communication challenges in the professional world. All series are client specific and tailored to meet your development need, specific to your industry. Audiences encompass all individuals in the organization at any level, from a basic course to an advanced version for the seasoned professional. Select a group of 20 participants that would benefit from specific topics.

Time of day options to consider:

Breakfast Eye-Opener
Lunch N Learn Opportunity
Morning or Afternoon Refresher

Suggested Topics:

Controlling Anxiety – Is it stage fright, if there is no stage?
Audience Analysis – Who is in the audience, what are their needs?
Present with a Purpose – Organize your message from start to finish.
I can read you like a book – The power of non-verbal body language
Tackling Scientific or Financial presentations – Difficult topics made easy and interesting.
What did you say? – How to handle difficult questions and objections
10 Steps to Professional Presence – What is it and is it necessary in the workplace?
You Inc. – Who are you and what message, are you sending to others?
Myths of Public Speaking – Do you really have to make them laugh?
Are you listening? – How well do you listen and retain information?
Business Etiquette 101 – Should you email, text or hand write a message?
Other – your choice

Recommended Plan:

Choose a series of 3 – 5 topics that would make a difference in your organization. A consultant is available to assist and recommend a series for your specific challenges. The time and location is your choice.
