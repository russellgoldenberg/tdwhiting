"Think like a wise man, but speak in the language of the people." - William Butler Yeats

What is Presentation Skills Training?

Having extensive knowledge of a subject matter does not equate with communicating it effectively. Your knowledge remains dormant, unless you feel comfortable, and confident with yourself, your material and your audience.

This 2-day, highly interactive program focuses on professional business communication.

All participants are videotaped. This exercise expedites the learning process and allows for extensive oral and written feedback for you to use and implement immediately. The workshop is delivered on-site at time and location of your choice. All programs are client specific designed to meet your development needs, specific to your industry.

Target Audience: The target audience is any individual who wants to increase their comfort level, manage anxiety, appear organized and exude confidence and credibility when presenting. A novice presenter or an experienced executive who presents often, will increase and strengthen their communication skills, guaranteed. Enroll up to 6 participants per consultant.

Suggested Topics to be covered:

Listed below are the most common challenges professionals face when preparing for a presentation or meeting.

 Manage anxiety (Physical and Emotional)
 Present with polish (Do you exude comfort, confidence and credibility?)
 Know your audience (Do they know you?)
 Choose the right words (Reduce fillers, i.e. um, uh. Increase tentative language awareness)
 Mind map your message (Meet your brain, be concise)
Organize your thoughts (Where do you begin & how do you conclude?)
 Use humor effectively and cautiously (Are you comfortable telling a joke, should you?)
 Powerful non verbal body language (It’s not what you say, it’s how you say it)
 Handling difficult questions and objections (Is there a heckler out there?)
 Are you listening? (Do you remember what your colleagues and manager told you?)
 Proper use of a podium and microphone (Can you move away from a podium?)
 Increase your comfort level on a panel (Can you perform without a script?)
 Can technical, scientific & financial presentations be interesting (Get & keep their attention)

Recommended Process:

An agenda is proposed for approval. The needs assessment determines the length of the program. Presentation Skills is typically 2 days.
